Who is Joshua Young?



Joshua Young is a freshman at Duke University. He is involved in Duke Robotics, the Jewish Student Union, and the Innovation Co-Lab. He graduated in 2016 from Indian Hill High School in the top 10 of his class. He played Varsity Soccer and was involved in the Latin Club, robotics club, and science olympiad. He also was the Editor of the Ohio Junior Classical League and on the leadership council of the INTERalliance of Greater Cincinnati. During the summers, he interned in Software Development at the Western & Southern Financial Group and IT at the University of Cincinnati.

He makes the most out of every opportunity. When he decides to do something, he completes it. Using these skills, he hopes to lead a technology company in the future.
